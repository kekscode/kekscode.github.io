By Stefan Antoni

March 25, 2016

I did a cleanup

I threw away every other blog or static webpage i had and started new. This page - http://stefan.antoni.io - is the only personal webpage i maintain right now. Everything else has been shut down.

I did also switch from german language to english.

This means:

  1. No headaches anymore because of a makeshift static site generator
  2. No fiddling with multi-page deployment workflows anymore
  3. Hopefully reaching a broader audience

I feel pretty excited right now.
